### Metrics Provided
1. **Accuracy** - How often correct (%)
2. **Precision** - When predicting PASS, how often right (%)
3. **Recall** - Of actual PASS cases, how many found (%)
4. **F1-Score** - Balance of precision and recall
5. **MAE** - Average error (points)
